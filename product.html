PRODUCT PAGE

Treadmill:

  • A treadmill is a popular cardio fitness equipment.
  • It consists of a moving conveyor belt where users can walk, jog, or run.
  • Treadmills often have adjustable speed and incline settings for varying workout intensities.

Dumbbells:

  • Dumbbells are versatile free weights used for strength training.
  • They come in various weights, allowing users to customize their workouts.
  • Dumbbells are essential for exercises like bicep curls, shoulder presses, and lunges.

Stationary Bike:

  • Stationary bikes are cardio machines that simulate cycling.
  • >They provide a low-impact workout option, suitable for users of different fitness levels.
  • Stationary bikes often come with adjustable resistance levels and built-in workout programs.

Resistance Bands:

  • Resistance bands are elastic bands used for strength training.
  • They offer resistance in various exercises, helping to target specific muscle groups.
  • Resistance bands are portable, making them suitable for home workouts as well.

Rowing Machine:

  • Rowing machines provide a full-body workout by simulating rowing movements.
  • They engage muscles in the arms, legs, back, and core.
  • Rowing machines often have adjustable resistance settings for different fitness levels.

Fitness Ball:

  • Fitness balls, also known as stability or exercise balls, are used for core exercises.
  • They help improve balance, stability, and flexibility.
  • Fitness balls can be incorporated into exercises like planks, squats, and ball crunches.
